About Top Tier Custom Floors

Precision craftsmanship. Real materials. No shortcuts.

The Craftsman

Stone Peters

Stone Peters founded Top Tier Custom Floors on a principle that most contractors have forgotten: the details matter more than the deadline. Every tread, every joint, every transition gets the same level of attention whether it's a single-room refinish or a three-story custom hardwood installation.

Based in Valley Center, California, Stone works directly with homeowners and builders across San Diego County and North Orange County. No subcontractors on the critical work. No cutting corners to save time. Just precision installation backed by years of experience working with the best materials in the industry.

Top Tier Custom Floors is headquartered in Valley Center, CA and serves the full corridor from San Diego County through North Orange County. We regularly work in Escondido, San Marcos, Carlsbad, Oceanside, Rancho Santa Fe, Del Mar, La Jolla, Poway, and across San Diego proper.
