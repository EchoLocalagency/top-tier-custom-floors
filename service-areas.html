Headquarters

Valley Center, CA

Top Tier Custom Floors is based in Valley Center, in the heart of North County San Diego. From here, we serve homeowners and builders across the full corridor from southern San Diego County up through North Orange County.

We handle projects of all sizes, from single-room refinishes to full-home custom hardwood installations spanning multiple floors. If you're within our service area and need precision flooring work, give us a call.
