Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you contact us through our website, phone, or social media, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, property address, and project details. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and provide estimates for our flooring services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Schedule and complete flooring projects
  • Communicate about your project status
  • Improve our services

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This service collects anonymous data about page views, time on site, and general location. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites such as Instagram and Facebook. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Data Security
