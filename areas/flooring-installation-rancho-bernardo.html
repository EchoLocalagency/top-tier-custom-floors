Top Tier Custom Floors provides professional flooring installation throughout Rancho Bernardo and the surrounding communities. From a single room to a full-home transformation, we treat your space like it is our own.

Our Services

Flooring Services Available in Rancho Bernardo

We offer the full range of residential and commercial flooring services.

Hardwood Flooring

Solid and engineered hardwood installation with premium species including white oak, hickory, maple, and walnut. Nail-down, glue-down, and floating methods.

Herringbone & Patterns

Custom herringbone, chevron, basketweave, and inlay patterns that elevate any room. Precision-cut and installed for a flawless geometric design.

Metallic Epoxy

High-gloss metallic epoxy coatings for garages, showrooms, and commercial spaces. Durable, seamless, and visually striking.

Polished Concrete

Diamond-polished concrete floors for a sleek, modern, low-maintenance surface. Ideal for commercial and contemporary residential spaces.

Stair Installation

Custom stair treads, risers, nosings, and LED stair lighting. Match your stairs to your new or existing floors.

Sand & Refinish

Dustless sanding, custom staining, and refinishing to restore worn hardwood floors. Oil-based, water-based, and hardwax oil finishes.

Laminate & Vinyl

Laminate, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), and VCT installation. Waterproof options for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial spaces.
Why Top Tier

Why Rancho Bernardo Homeowners Choose Us

Many homes in Rancho Bernardo were built in the 1980s and 1990s, which means original flooring is ready for an update. We frequently replace worn carpet and dated tile with hardwood or LVP in these homes. The slab-on-grade construction common here is well-suited for glue-down engineered hardwood or floating LVP. We understand the HOA considerations that come with condo and townhome projects in this area.

Every project is handled personally by the owner. There are no subcontractors, no sales teams, and no middlemen. When you call Top Tier Custom Floors, you are talking to the person who will measure your space, help you select materials, and install your floors. That level of accountability is rare in this industry, and it is the reason our clients refer us to their neighbors.

We use premium materials from trusted brands and suppliers, and we never cut corners on subfloor preparation. The work that happens before the first plank goes down is what determines how your floor looks and performs five, ten, and twenty years from now. We take that seriously.
